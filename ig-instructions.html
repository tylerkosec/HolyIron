Getting Your Instagram
Feed on the Website

We're showing your live Instagram feed directly on the Holy Iron website so clients can see your work without leaving. Here's what we need from you — it takes about 5 minutes.

01
Switch to a Professional Account

In Instagram, go to Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account. Choose Creator. This is free and doesn't change how your account looks to anyone.

02
Connect a Facebook Page

You'll need a Facebook Page linked to your Instagram. You can create a simple one in a few minutes — it doesn't have to be active or public-facing. Go to Instagram Settings → Account → Linked Accounts → Facebook.

03
Send Tyler a Message

Once steps 1 and 2 are done, just text or DM Tyler. He'll handle the rest — generating the token and plugging it into the site. You don't need to do anything technical.

Why does this matter?
Right now your feed shows placeholder tiles on the site. Once this is set up, your actual posts show up automatically — every new post you make appears on the site. No extra work from you.
